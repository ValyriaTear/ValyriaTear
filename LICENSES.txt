Licenses
========

Last update: 2018.04.09

This file is listing all the files licenses from the point of the fork
from the Allacrost project.
Note: The licensing of the allacrost's files is assumed to be GPLv2 or later,
as told on the allacrost web site:
https://allacrost.org/

'OGA' stands for: Open Game Art - https://opengameart.org
'dA' stands for: Deviant Art - https://deviantart.com
'The Mana World' or 'TMW' is referring to: https://www.themanaworld.org

GPLv2+ or GPLv3+ refer to assets under the standard license with the
"or any later version" text.  "GPLv2" markings are for assets where it
is unclear whether the "or any later version" should be present or
not, and we will mark any content that definitely doesn't have the
text as "GPLv2-only".  "GPLv2/3" refers to content (from OGA) that
explicitly lists GPLv2 or v3 but doesn't clarify whether the "or any
later version" text should be present.
